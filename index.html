Welcome to our website.........

Capuchin Franciscan Friars have been ministering in Ethiopia, as ‘Brothers of the People’ for morethan 100 years. On this website you can find information about where we are, who we are and how to get in touch. You will also find information on our day to day activities as well as how to follow a Capuchin Vocation. Thank you for taking the time to visit us.

Our Values:

Creating a safe and welcoming environments where the gifts of all are cherished

Building peace by treating all creation fairly and promoting structural change for a more just order

Encouraging people to realize and respect their own dignity and potential.
